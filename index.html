Retro Tunes
>
Retro music, "or the oldies," had a huge influence on future of music. It even created newer genres, allowing people to experience different aspects of music and become more creative or experimental with their tastes. This has resulted in the mass popularisation of many artists and their music.​

Nowadays, teens and young adults often find themselves listening to more oldies or retro-style music from modern artists, due to the nostalgic and comforting vibes, that older music give off.​

Join us as we get to know more about these retro tunes.
